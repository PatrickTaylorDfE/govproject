Census dates

Find out when the school census will open for each term.

Every school in England has a statutory duty to complete the school census each term.

The 2019 to 2020 census dates are:

  • autumn census
    • census date – Thursday 3 October 2019
    • return date – Wednesday 30 October 2019
  • spring census
    • census date – Thursday 16 January 2020
    • return date – Wednesday 12 February 2020
  • summer census
    • census date – Thursday 21 May 2020
    • return date – Wednesday 17 June 2020

If you are a maintained school or pupil referral units, you should contact your local authority to find out their deadlines for submission.

You can download a spreadsheet of all the information we ask for if you need it.
